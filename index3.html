Tusheti

Located on the northern slopes of the Greater Caucasus Mountains, Tusheti is bordered by the Russian republics of Chechnya and Dagestan to the north and east, respectively; and by the Georgian historic provinces Kakheti and Pshav-Khevsureti to the south and west, respectively.[1] The population of the area is mainly ethnic Georgians called Tushs or Tushetians (Georgian: tushebi).
