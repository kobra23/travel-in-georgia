About Dashbash

Rti - is a domed building of the "Free Cross" type of St. George. According to the inscription, X-XI centuries. They were erected on the border by the order of Eristav-Eristavi Rati of Trialeti. The building is damaged. Only fragments of the cornice are left from the neck of the dome. Along with the faces spread in the decorative jewelry of the building, there are also original sculptural compositions.
